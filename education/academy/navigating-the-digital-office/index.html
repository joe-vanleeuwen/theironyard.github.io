Navigating the Digital Office

Become a technology power user

What you will learn and do

Almost every part of our jobs are being digitized, and keeping up with the curve is difficult. If you’ve ever been frustrated with email, PDFs, or other programs on your computer, this class is for you. We’ll teach you proven tricks and tips to become 10 times more efficient on your computer and online.

Curriculum Overview

  • We teach skills for Mac and PC!
  • Gmail power user tricks (process, labels, and more!)
  • Outlook power user tricks (process, categories, and more!)
  • Email tools to declutter your inbox and clean up your to do list
  • Mastering your calendar
  • All things PDF: how to make them, edit them, convert them, reduce file size, send them, and more
  • Office crash-course: power user tips for Word, Excel, and PPT
  • Images: resolution, resizing, and why it matters
  • To-do lists: the best apps to help your productivity

Excited? You’ve come to the right place.

Is this for you?

Whether you need to start with the basics or sharpen your digital business skills, this class will help you learn latest and greatest in using tech to your advantage.

Are there prerequisite skills?

You should have basic computer literacy and typing skills. You will need to bring your own computer (or iPad).
