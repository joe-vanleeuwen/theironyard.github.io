Intro to iPhone Development

Get started developing iOS apps

What you will learn and do

Today apps are the standard for how people consume, share, and create information. We want to help you dip your toes into the water of what it takes to build those apps. In this one-day class you’ll get setup and started making apps for the iPhone and iPad.

Curriculum Overview

  • Introduction Objective-C
  • How to register for your Apple developer’s account
  • How to get started with Xcode
  • All about Apple’s design guidelines
  • The steps required to distribute your app to beta users
  • What’s required to submit your app to Apple for review

Excited? You’ve come to the right place.

Is this for you?

Ideally you have a background in software development…any language…but you’ll move faster if you’ve seen C, C++, or Java.

Are there prerequisite skills?

You’ll need to bring an Apple computer for this hands-on class.
