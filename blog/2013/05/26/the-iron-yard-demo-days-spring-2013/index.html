The Iron Yard Demo Days - Spring 2013

Posted May 26th, 2013

Categories: Accelerator

After three amazing, difficult, and rewarding months, our Spring 2013 Accelerator teams are ready to share their products with the world—and investors.

This year we’ll launch on our home turf at the Grok conference, then hit the road and head to west, northeast, and back into the heartland of the south for pitch sessions.

We’d love for you to join us at any point along the way. Here’s a list of our demo days. Scroll down for details.

  • May 30th @ 2pm EST – Demo Day at the Grok Conference (RSVP)
  • June 3rd @ 10:30am PST – Morning Demo Session at Silicon Valley Bank (RSVP)
  • June 3rd @ 3:30pm PST – Afternoon Demo Session at Silicon Valley Bank (RSVP)
  • June 4th @ various times – Launch: Silicon Valley (Register)
  • June 11th @ 10:00am EST – Demo Day in Boston (RSVP)
  • June 12-13 @ various times EST – Startup Village at Southland Startup Conference (Register)
